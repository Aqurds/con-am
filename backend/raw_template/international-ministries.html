What is AGWM International Ministries?

AGWM International Ministries (IM) is a network of strategic ministries meeting specific needs for missionaries and national fellowships worldwide. IM missionaries are uniquely skilled in providing materials, training, and other on-the-ground assistance for missionary outreach. Using printed resources, one-on-one training, construction expertise, medical assistance, and innovative technologies, International Ministries helps fulfill the overall AGWM mission to reach the lost, plant churches, train disciples, and serve the poor and suffering.

This ministry network is led by veteran missionary, Dr. JoAnn Butrin. Each ministry is staffed by skilled and passionate missions personnel, many with years of field experience, cultural adaptation, language expertise, and specific giftings. IM missionaries are passionate about giving every person everywhere a chance to learn about Jesus Christ, respond to His invitation to experience a relationship with Him, and to join a community of faith as a life transformed.

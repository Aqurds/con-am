Assemblies of God World Mission

Our Missional Strategy

Fundamental to Assemblies of God World Mission’s outlook is our commitment to establishing the Church among all people everywhere. We do that by reaching, planting, training, and serving. These four categories of ministry actions support the breadth of God’s work. They are rarely separate acts or processes but woven together in the work of our global teams and partners. So all can hear the hope of the gospel.

reaching planting tranining serving

Reaching

Following the witness of scripture, we seek to proclaim the good news where Jesus has not been named. The great sobering reality is that the message of Christ has not been presented to more than two - thirds of the world’s population. Our commitment is that under the guidance of the Spirit, we will expendevery effort through every effective means to proclaim the message of Jesus to those who do not know Him.

Planting

We believe that sustainable growth in the Kingdom rests on the planting of healthy churches. The Spirit-reliant church flourishes as it depends on the Spirit to empower its work among its local context. These indigenous churches are self-supporting, self-propagating, and self-governing. Our goal is national church movements that plant churches and send cross-cultural workers.

Training

Assemblies of God World Missions trains spiritual leaders to serve effectively as pastors, evangelists, teachers, and missionaries. Following the discipleship patterns of Jesus Christ and the early church, we train emerging leaders to undertake the Great Commission. Historically, AGWM has focused on training in community using localized quality resources. Spirit-empowered leaders bring up Spirit-empowered leaders that work to establish His Church.

Serving

We partner with local believers and churches to serve poor and suffering people with the compassion of Christ and invite them to become His followers. Assemblies of God missionaries, national churches, and compassion ministries serve people’s physical needs. We believe the words of Jesus: “For what does it profit a man to gain the whole world, and forfeit his soul?” Yet Jesus also commanded us not to ignore the suffering of others. We integrate compassion ministry into sharing the gospel and establishing the Church.
